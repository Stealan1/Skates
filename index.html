The Flow

Set it up once. Run forever.

SkatesD2RHub is built around a clean setup-once, run-always workflow. From adding your first account to cycling your tenth game in a row — every step is exactly where you'd expect it.

01
Add Your Accounts

Add all your Battle.net accounts — by password or token. As many as you have. The built-in Token Helper gets you a valid token in under a minute if you need one.

02
Build Your Teams

Group accounts into named teams and add them to your Quick Launch. Assign each slot a role — Main, BO Barb, Filler, or Creator — before you ever hit Load. You can also define your own custom roles.

03
Roles Ready Before Launch

Everything is pre-configured in the team setup — including the optional Creator role, which lets a high-level filler handle game creation and spawn a higher player count instead of the main account.

04
Hit Load → First Game

Press Load and the team launches straight into the first game — no manual window juggling. Battle Orders fires, leeches join, the Creator handles spawning if assigned. You're in and running immediately.

05
Create or Follow

Your team doesn't have to create every game. Switch into Follow mode and your accounts will join an existing game instead — useful when someone else is hosting, or you're running alongside another session.

06
New Game, Instantly

Back out, create, rejoin — in seconds. Hit New Game and the whole team is back in a fresh lobby before you've taken three steps from the entrance. A run counter keeps everything in sequence.

What It Does

Every Feature You Actually Need

No bloat. Just clean, fast, reliable D2R automation that gets out of your way and lets you play.

Up to 8 Windows at Once

Run up to 8 D2R instances simultaneously — each one a separate account, all moving as a single coordinated team. One game holds up to 8 players, and SkatesD2RHub fills every slot.

Core Feature
7+ Speed Modes — Fully Configurable

Seven speed presets built in, from cautious and steady all the way to flat-out. Each mode controls how fast the automation runs between actions — and if none of them fit exactly, you can tune the timing yourself directly in the app.

Performance
EU · NA · KR Realms

Full support across all three Battle.net realms. Each team slot can be locked to a specific server, or set to "Ask" mode to choose on the fly — no reconfiguring needed.

Multi-Realm
Create or Follow

Your team can create games, or follow into an existing one — your choice per session. Follow mode is perfect when someone else is hosting or you're running alongside a second team.

Flexibility
Humanized Timing

Every click uses natural coordinate randomization and sleep jitter — making the automation look and behave like a real player. No robotic precision patterns.

Safety
Battle Orders — Hands Free

Your BO Barb casts Battle Orders on the full team automatically — without touching the main screen or interrupting your flow. It fires, confirms, and gets out of the way.

Team Play
Low Ressource Mods

Two mods are bundled that reduce the game's resource load, making D2R run leaner when you have multiple instances open. Both are free, and the app downloads and installs them for you automatically — no manual setup.

Performance
Handle Killing — Built Right In

Clearing D2R's instance mutex (handle killing) is handled entirely inside the app — no third-party tools to download, no command line. It runs automatically when you need a clean instance launch.

Macro Maker

Record Any Route. Play It Back Forever.

The Macro Maker lets you click through any sequence inside D2R and save it as a reusable macro. No scripting. No coordinates to memorize. Just click, save, and bind a key.

Assign a hotkey, assign which roles should run it, and it fires exactly as recorded — every single time. Dividers split your macro into chunks for round-robin cycling across runs.

  • Click-record directly inside any live D2R window
  • Edit, reorder, or delete individual steps after recording
  • Add dividers to break a long route into per-run chunks
  • Assign macros to specific team roles from the Overview tab
